Who am I?

Hello fellow DevOps enthusiasts! My name is Stoyan Rahnev and I am 27 years old. Originally from Burgas, love and dreams led me to currently reside in Sofia. Currently a happy father of a girl. From younger age I had always passion for computers. As a personality, I am open-minded, friendly and very hard working.

My Journey

My journey began 2017 when I was a first grader in university. I had the rare chance to start working as a part-time Junior Technical Support in one IT company. Few years later I have decided to continue my journey in different company, which led me to my job at Silver Star. There I started as a Technical Support, a year later promoted to System Administrator and few years later to where I am now - Data Security Officer.
